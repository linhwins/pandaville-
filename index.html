Pandaville

  • High Concept: Use up/down/left/right arrows to allow panda to jump on platforms and obtain coins.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the players to have a fun, relaxing experience when playing the game.
  • Genre: This game can definitely be a mobile app game or an easy online game for kids.
  • Game play: Player can use their cursors (up/down/left/right) arrows to allow movement for the panda. As the panda collides with the coins, the coins will dissapear. The game will end when all coins are gone. The panda was given a high velocity when jumping in order to reach multiple platforms. The prototype's expected effects were much similiar. The goal is to obtain all the coins.
  • Prototype goal: The protype is evaluating velocity and gravity of the character as well as collision within objects.
  • Features: Some unique aspects that set this game is part is that its very user-friendly. The game is easy to play and contains cute, cartoon like graphics. The game is also versatile. It can easily be produced and played on a mobile or ipad device.
  • Setting/Premise: The game is set in a village called Pandaville. The leader, Jumbo is in charged of all the pandas and whom everyone look up to. One night, while all the pandas were sleeping, zookeepers invaded Pandaville and kidnapped all the pandas to the zoo. However, Jumbo had his own hut and was able to escape through all that chaos. Now he must collect these coins and find a way to rescue his people from the zookeper. The coins will serve as token to free his people.
  • Story: The game is set in a village called Pandaville. All the pandas were taken into a local zoo, however the main panda(Jumbo) managed to escape and have to collect coins in order to rescue his village. The coins serve as tokens to free his people from the zoo. The zookeeper made a deal with Jumbo and will release his people if he brings her all the coins. If Jumbo cant collect all the coins, his peole will be stuck in the zoo forever! :(
  • Target Audience: The game targets children! And maybe even adults!
  • Play time: The game takes about less than a minute to play.
  • Strategies: Some player strategies expected are motor skills and eye coordination.
  • Prototype Assets: This game is a little similar to the characteristics of Phaser ninja physics.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: A game that might resemble my game would be Mario.
